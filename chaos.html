How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles touch, they merge into one!, depending on their ratio between one another.

When two tiles with a ratio between 0 to 1 collide, they merge into one. Two neutral zero tiles will merge together to form one 0 tile, and would just occupy a space on the grid.

When one positive and one negative tile collide, they merge into one to form a Unicode character on the logarithmic scale, starting with A (Unicode character #65, or 0041.). i.e. 2 and -2 (2ยน) 3 and -3 (1.5ร2ยน) will merge to form A, regardless of the base number.

When two Unicode characters of the same kind touch they merge into a tile with the next Unicode character! For a challenge, try to beat the game by going to 10FFFF!
